2. **Market Viability**: 
   - The necessity to assess technology efficiency and market potential, including understanding customer needs and competitive landscape.
   - Identifying potential barriers to market entry and strategies to overcome them.

3. **Regulatory Hurdles**: 
   - Navigating complex regulatory frameworks that govern waste management and energy production.
   - Ensuring compliance with local, state, and federal regulations to avoid legal pitfalls.

4. **Community Acceptance**: 
   - Gaining the support of local communities, which is crucial for the project's success.
   - Addressing public concerns regarding environmental impact and health risks associated with waste-to-energy facilities.
